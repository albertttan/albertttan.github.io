Poem of the Day 每日诗歌

Sonnet
Beigou

Shall I compare thee to a bagel's grace?
Thou art more tempting in thy circular space:
With golden crust and tender, chewy core,
Oh bagel, thou art a delight to adore!

The aroma of baking fills the air,
With cream cheese or jam, beyond compare.
Morning's beginning, lunchtime's delight, In your presence,
the world feels just right.

In New York or Montreal, you're renowned,
Countless flavors and shapes to be found.
Single or double, it matters not at all,
You're the crown jewel of breakfast, standing tall.

Whether whole wheat or plain,
you shine so bright, Your charm can't be dimmed, your taste takes flight.
You'll never wither, you'll never grow old,
Your delicious legacy, forever told.

十四行诗
贝狗

我是否可以把你比喻成贝果？
就如早晨的阳光一般明媚：
金黄的外皮，内部柔软松脆，
贝果啊，你是美味的艺术品！

烘烤的香气填满了整个厨房，
搭配奶酪或果酱，总是美味；
早晨的开始，午餐的享受，
你是一天中最美妙的时刻。

在纽约或蒙特利尔，你流行无边，
各种口味和形状，不计其数；
无论是一层或两层，你都令人陶醉，
你是早餐的皇冠，无人可及。

无论是全麦还是百吉，无所谓，
你的魅力无法被忽视，无法阻挡；
永远不会褪去，永远不会凋零，
你的美味将永远在人们的唇上流传。

On This Day 以史为鉴

December 15
12月15日：荷兰王国日（1954年）、柴门霍夫日（世界语文化）、世界猫奴日

  • 687 – Sergius was elected pope, ending the last disputed period of sede vacante during the Byzantine Papacy.
  • 1025 – Constantine VIII became the sole Byzantine emperor, 63 years after being crowned co-emperor.
  • 1467 – Troops under Stephen III of Moldavia defeated the forces of Matthias Corvinus of Hungary at the Battle of Baia in present-day Romania.
  • 1890 – Sitting Bull, a Hunkpapa Lakota leader, was killed on Standing Rock Indian Reservation in South Dakota by U.S. Indian agency police.
  • 1981 – The Iraqi Shia Islamist group al-Dawa carried out one of the first modern suicide bombings, targeting the Iraqi embassy in Beirut, Lebanon, resulting in 61 deaths and at least 100 injuries.
  • 1791年 – 《美国宪法》第一条至第十条修正案获得批准，后来统称为“美国权利法案”。
  • 1891年 – 美国基督教青年会国际训练学校体育教师詹姆斯·奈史密斯提出篮球运动的相关规则。
  • 1961年 – 纳粹德国高阶官员阿道夫·艾希曼因战争罪与危害人类罪等15项罪名而遭判处死刑。
  • 1970年 – 苏联空间探测器金星7号成功于金星表面着陆，成为史上第一个于其他行星成功着陆的探测器。
  • 1980年 – 台湾新竹科学工业园区正式成立，其后逐渐发展为全球主要高科技产品代工重镇。

Births and Deaths: David Teniers the Younger (bapt. 1610); Florence Jepperson Madsen (b. 1886); Vallabhbhai Patel (d. 1950)
